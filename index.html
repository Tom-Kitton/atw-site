A Traveller's Way
The Play-by-Post game of Mystery and Exploration
A Traveler's Way is a role-playing game that focuses on collaborative world-building and immersion. Players tell their story in concert with an Administrator, who determines the outcomes of their actions using a rules-light resolution system which encourages creativity, commitment to character, and lateral thinking.
Texts
Newspapers, Political Playsheets, Scandal Sheets, Leaflets, Ephemera, Gazettes, Works of Rerference, Surgical Manuals.
Character Creation
Our character generator, biographies of famous criminals, genealogies of prominent families, children's stories regarding what they did on feast days.
Starting the Game
Obtain papers of passage for your character and take your first steps on the highroad.
