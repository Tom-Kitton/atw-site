A Traveller's Way
The Play-by-Post game of Mystery and Exploration
A Traveler's Way is a role-playing game that focuses on collaborative world-building and immersion. Players tell their story in concert with an Administrator, who determines the outcomes of their actions using a rules-light resolution system which encourages creativity, commitment to character, and lateral thinking.
Setting Texts
Newspapers, Political Playsheets, Scandal Sheets, Leaflets, Ephemera, Gazettes, Works of Rerference, Surgical Manuals, literary reviews, stockmarket reports, instructions on the best cooking of grouse.
Character Creation
Our character generator, biographies of famous criminals, genealogies of prominent families, children's stories regarding what they did on feast days.
Starting the Game
Obtain papers of passage for your character and take your first steps on the highroad.
Random Generators
Determine plot twists, create scandals, imagine the contents of wondrous emporia, author book titles, generate newspaper headlines, populate villages and towns, variously otherwise meddle with fate and chance on a scale that beggars belief etc.
Illustrations
Travellers' impressions of striking prospects and vantages, portraits of distinguides and everyday persons, architectural sketches, heraldic ensigns, lunatic daubings, engravings composed by prodigious blind sorcerors and children's scratching depicting dogs with wings.
Beta Rules
Sketches towards a classical, tabletop implementation of A Traveller's Way so as to make fuller use of my collection of dice and impose on the evenings of august persons.
